FAQs

Get Clarifications on Game Rules, Features, and More

Q: How is the score calculated in the quiz?

A: Each correct answer earns you 1 point. At the end of the quiz, your total score is calculated based on the number of correct answers you provided.

Q: Can I change my answer after submitting?

A: No, once you have submitted your answer, it cannot be changed. So make sure to review your answer before submitting it.

Q: Are there any hints or lifelines available in the quiz?

A: No, the quiz does not provide any hints or lifelines. It's a test of your knowledge and decision-making skills.

Q: Can I see my score during the quiz?

A: No, your score will be revealed at the end of the quiz once you have completed all the questions.

Q: Can I retake the quiz?

A: Yes, you can retake the quiz as many times as you like. Challenge yourself to improve your score with each attempt!

Q: Can I play the game on my mobile device?

A: Yes, the game is fully compatible with mobile devices. You can play it on your smartphone or tablet.
